# 🏀 Sports Card Tracker

A simple and elegant web app to track your sports card collection, monitor purchases and sales, and calculate profits.

## Features

✅ **Add Cards** - Log cards you purchase with:
- Player name
- Year and card set
- Card condition (Mint, Near Mint, Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor)
- Purchase price and date

✅ **Track Sales** - Record when you sell cards and instantly calculate:
- Profit or loss per card
- Total revenue from sales
- Overall profit/loss on your collection

✅ **View Collection** - Browse all your cards with filtering options:
- All cards
- Owned cards only
- Sold cards only

✅ **Collection Stats** - See at-a-glance metrics:
- Total number of cards
- Cards currently owned
- Cards sold
- Total amount invested
- Total revenue from sales
- Overall profit/loss

✅ **Local Storage** - All data is saved locally in your browser
- No account required
- Your data persists between sessions
- All data stays on your device

## How to Use

1. **Add a Card**: Go to the "Add Card" tab and fill in your card details
2. **View Collection**: Check out all your cards in the "My Collection" tab
3. **Sell a Card**: Click "Sell Card" on any card you own to record a sale
4. **Check Stats**: Visit the "Stats" tab to see your collection performance

## Getting Started

1. Clone or download this repository
2. Open `index.html` in your web browser
3. Start adding your sports cards!

## Technology

- **CSS3** - Styling and responsive design
